Introduction Work Integrated learning (WIL) a crucial opportunity for students to be able to form a bridge between the knowledge we gain in the classroom in a theoretical manner and actually put into practice in a real-world setting. The purpose of WIL is to provide students with exposure to the industry in its authentic sense, the environment, where academic concepts can be applied, practical skills can be developed and students gain insight into real-world standards and expectations.
